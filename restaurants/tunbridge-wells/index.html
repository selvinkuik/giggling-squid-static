Find Our Restaurant

Located in the quieter end of the High Street, our restaurant is an oasis of calm within a few minutes’ walk of shopping destinations – Royal Victoria Place and Pantiles – and the Odeon cinema and Trinity Theatre.

About Tunbridge Wells

Enjoy stylish dining with a Thai twist – a friendly, relaxed atmosphere where the focus is on sharing plenty of delicious dishes with friends and family and having a giggle or two.

Perfect Occasion

Quirky, characterful and welcoming, our Tunbridge restaurant is a great place for lunch with friends or colleagues or a long, lingering Thai feast for dinner at the weekend.

Private Dining

Our lovely restaurant has an entire upstairs section available for private hire if you’re looking to celebrate that special occasion with a full Thai feast or just cocktails and nibbles!

Dishing the delicious with authentic Thai ingredients

Each dish – from our famous Salt & Pepper Squid to crowd-pleasing Sticky Chicken – is a work of love, exploring Thailand’s richness of natural ingredients from the land and sea and crafted by our expert Thai chefs.
