Located in the main thoroughfare of Beaconsfield, a charming Market Town with plenty of cultural interest including the UK’s oldest pub and Clivedon House nearby.

About Our Restaurant

One of our larger restaurants, our Beaconsfield Giggling Squid offers a spacious but welcoming interior inspired by the fresh, exotic Thai ingredients from land and sea that feature in our authentic, flavourful dishes.

Our Interiors

Our Beaconsfield restaurant is spacious, spreading across two floors but with plenty of quieter, intimate spaces which make it perfect for a dinner with friends or even a lunchtime business meeting over a meal.

Private Garden

Our stunning walled garden offers a spacious, tranquil area perfect for outdoor dining with plenty of seating including our fairytale birdcages. Book early when the weather is favourable to enjoy the atmosphere!

Dishing the delicious with authentic Thai ingredients

Each dish – from our famous Salt & Pepper Squid to crowd-pleasing Sticky Chicken – is a work of love, exploring Thailand’s richness of natural ingredients from the land and sea and crafted by our expert Thai chefs.
