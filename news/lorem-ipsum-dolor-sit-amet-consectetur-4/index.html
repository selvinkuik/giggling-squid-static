6 Adults
45 minutes
Easy
The pancake
  • 100g Pandan Leaves
  • 5 Drops of Natural Yellow Food Colouring
  • 1 Yolk of an Egg
  • 300g Plain Flour
  • 2g Salt
  • 50mls Evaporated Milk
  • 300mls Water
  • 1/2 Very Ripe Banana
The garnish
  • 100g Pandan Leaves
  • 1 Yolk of an Egg

Summary

This recipe creates lots of little pancakes (up to 20), but as they are small- you may need a few each! These taste sweet, soft and creamy and we think they’re just amazing.

Method

Pancake mix
  1. Cut the pandan leaves up and blend them with a little water
  2. Drain pandan water into a jug, removing the remaining bits of leaf
  3. Put the jug of pandan water to one side for now…
  4. Mix together the plain flour, the yolk of the egg, the whole egg, sugar and salt into a large bowl
  5. Add pandan water, a small amount at a time, until the consistency is creamy
