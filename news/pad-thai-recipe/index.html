Pad Thai Recipe

2 ADULTS
45 mins
Moderate
Pad Thai Sauce:
  • 1/2 cup of tamarind pulp, extract or paste (you can find this at most large supermarkets in the continental aisle or buy this online)
  • 1/2 cup of palm or white sugar
  • ¼ red onion sliced
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of chilli powder
  • 1 tsp cooking oil
Pad Thai dish:
  • Two handfuls of soaked Pad Thai noodles (please prepare as per packet instructions)
  • 1 chicken breast thinly sliced (don’t for veggie recipe)
  • 10 pieces of fried tofu slices
  • 2 tbsp of red onion sliced
  • 6 tbsp Pad Thai sauce (from above)
  • 2 eggs (don’t add for vegan recipe)
  • 2 lime wedges
  • 2 tbsp chive leaves
  • Large handful beansprouts
  • 2 tbsp minced radish/finely diced carrots
  • 2 tbsp of veg stock
  • 2 tbsp of cooking oil
  • To garnish:
  • 2 coriander sprigs
  • 2 tbsp of crushed peanuts (not suitable for nut allergy sufferers.)

How to make the perfect Pad Thai

One of our most popular dishes, Pad Thai is always a crowd-pleaser. Not only delicious but surprisingly easy to cook from scratch with our (no longer secret) recipe below. Have fun giving it a go – giggles optional but recommended for extra flavour.

Make it veggie – don’t add the chicken.

Make it vegan – don’t add the chicken or the eggs.

This recipe includes peanuts as a garnish, which can easily be removed from the method if you have a nut allergy.

Method and Preparation

Preparing the sauce:
  1. Heat oil in a pan, add sliced red onions and fry until golden – give it a good stir.
  2. Next, add in the tamarind pulp, palm sugar and salt and cook, stirring constantly until the sauce thickens, has a deep colour and a nice gloss.
  3. Add the chilli powder – give it a little taste and adjust seasoning if you would live. You can cool it down and refrigerate until you need it for later.
Preparing the dish:
  1. Heat wok/pan with oil, add slicked chicken and fry until golden.
  2. Add 2 tbsp of sliced onions and cook. Then add whisked eggs and cook through.
  3. Add minced radish/finely diced carrots, fried tofu slices and cook further – keep on stirring.
  4. Add rice noodles followed by 4 tbsp of the Pad Thai sauce you created and fry together cooking the noodles.
  5. Add veg stock and fry further – don’t stop stirring.
  6. Next add another 1 tbsp of your Pad Thai sauce to the wok/pan and continue cooking.
  7. Finally add beansprouts, chive leaves and the remaining Pad Thai sauce to cover all of your ingredients.
Final dish assembly:
  1. Get a plate and dish up your food, you can garnish with a lime wedge and coriander.
  2. If you like you can add crushed peanuts to have on the side. (Please leave this step out if you have a nut allergy).
  3. Enjoy!
