Frequently asked questions

Here are some of our most common questions asked by you.

Is your meat Halal?
Yes we have Halal dishes. Our chicken and lamb is Halal across all our restaurants.
Can I have the main menu at lunchtime?
Yes, you can because what makes you happy, makes us happy. Just ask a member of staff for the All Day menu when you come in for lunch.
Do you have a lunch menu and what time is it avaliable?
Yes, we have a lunch menu that runs from midday until 5pm across most of our sites. Our lunch menu features our popular Thai tapas as well as some of our favourite curries and noodle dishes. See lunch menu here.
Do you have nutritional and calorie information available for your dishes?
Not currently but we’re working on it behind the scenes and calorie information will be available April 2022.
Do you cater for coeliacs?
Yes, many of our dishes gluten free. Those which contain gluten will be indicated with a G in the menu but it’s really important that you check our allergen guide to make sure your chosen dish doesn’t contain traces. Please check our menu here and our allergen guide here.
