Is your meat Halal?
Our chicken and lamb is Halal across all our restuarants.
Can I have the main menu at lunchtime?
Yes, you can because what makes you happy, makes us happy. Just ask a member of staff for the main menu when you come in for lunch.
Do you have a lunch menu and what time is it avaliable?
Yes, we have a lunch menu that runs from midday until 5pm across most of our sites. Our lunch menu features our popular Thai tapas as well as some of our favourite curries and noodle dishes.
Do you have nutritional and calorie information available for your dishes?
Not currently but we’re working on it behind the scenes and calorie information will be available April 2022.
Do you cater for coeliacs?
Yes, many of our dishes gluten free. Those which contain gluten will be indicated with a G in the menu but it’s really important that you check our allergen matrix to make sure your chosen dish doesn’t contain traces. Please check our menu here and our allergen matrix here.
