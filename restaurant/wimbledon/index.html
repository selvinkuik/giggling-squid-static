About Wimbledon

The inspiration for our botanical decor and flavour-rich dishes comes from Thailand’s extraordinary wealth of ingredients from the sea and land. Rooted in the ‘land of smiles’ you can expect warm, friendly service too.

We’re family friendly

There’s always a buzz in the Village and we love being part of it – meet your friends here for a midweek lunch post-shopping or treat your family to a traditional Thai feast, sharing dishes and giggles.

The perfect occasion

Us squids being famously flexible, our dining options are too! For that special occasion we can offer semi-private dining on the ground floor or you could book the whole of the top floor for an exclusive party!

Dishing the delicious with authentic Thai ingredients

Each dish – from our famous Salt & Pepper Squid to crowd-pleasing Sticky Chicken – is a work of love, exploring Thailand’s richness of natural ingredients from the land and sea and crafted by our expert Thai chefs.
