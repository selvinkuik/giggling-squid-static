Find our restaurant

Located just five minutes from Bristol city centre, our restaurant is ideally situated in the centre of Clifton Village within easy access of its must-see gardens, antique shops and secret hideaways.

About Clifton

Expect a dining experience to delight all the senses with our vibrant, botanical-inspired decor, friendly and welcoming service and our authentic, bold-flavoured Thai dishes, hot off the wok!

The perfect occasion

Our Clifton restaurant offers the kind of charming, welcoming and relaxed setting that suits all kinds of dining occasions from family lunches out with the children to cosy, romantic dinners for two.

Thai under the sky

If ‘Thai under the sky’ is where it’s at for a midweek lunch or romantic, weekend dinner – weather permitting – book one of our outdoor tables and enjoy people-watching over a selection of your Thai faves.

Dishing the delicious with authentic Thai ingredients

Each dish – from our famous Salt & Pepper Squid to crowd-pleasing Sticky Chicken – is a work of love, exploring Thailand’s richness of natural ingredients from the land and sea and crafted by our expert Thai chefs.
