About Brentwood

We’re all about delicious, authentic Thai food delivered with friendly, welcoming service. Our unique decor is vibrant with florals and bold colours creating a warm, inviting setting in which to enjoy a Thai feast.

Family friendly

Our relaxed, friendly atmosphere and range of menus including special, child-friendly dishes, make our Brentwood restaurant a great location for a family lunch on a fun day out or a catch up dinner after work.

Perfect occasion

Brentwood is the home town of ‘The Only Way is Essex’ – the question is, will you see any of the stars about when you visit us?

Dishing the delicious with authentic Thai ingredients

Each dish – from our famous Salt & Pepper Squid to crowd-pleasing Sticky Chicken – is a work of love, exploring Thailand’s richness of natural ingredients from the land and sea and crafted by our expert Thai chefs.
