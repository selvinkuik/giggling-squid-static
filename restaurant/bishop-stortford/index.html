Find us in the centre of the town, within a few minutes’ walk of the Jackson Square Shopping Centre and Waytemore Castle Gardens, and a brisk stroll away from the train station.

About Bishop’s Stortford

Think light, bright and bursting with colour! Our stylish decor echoes Thailand’s wealth of natural ingredients and gorgeous blooms – the same abundance and variety which inspires all our delicious Thai dishes.

Private booking

A great place for lunch or dinner, our spacious restaurant offers lovely nooks for an intimate meal or larger tables including our eight-man booth if you’re looking to celebrate a birthday or other special occasion.

The perfect occasion

We’re close to Stansted airport so if you’re heading to sunnier climes, stop in for a lunch or dinner. And for those special occasions, why not book one of our stunning, instragramable birdcages?

Dishing the delicious with authentic Thai ingredients

Each dish – from our famous Salt & Pepper Squid to crowd-pleasing Sticky Chicken – is a work of love, exploring Thailand’s richness of natural ingredients from the land and sea and crafted by our expert Thai chefs.
