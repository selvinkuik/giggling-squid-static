About Berkhamsted

Set on the High Street, an eight minute walk from the train station, we’re ideally placed to explore the sights – from the Rex Cinema with its famous art-deco auditorium to scenic canals and Berkhamsted Castle.

We welcome you

Cosy, warm and welcoming, with exposed beams and an open fireplace, our Berkhamsted restaurant is all about warmth and comfort from our friendly service to our hearty, happy-making Thai dishes.

Outdoor seating

When the weather’s being good to us, you can enjoy our ‘secret garden’ with its pretty fairylights, parasols and plenty of outdoor dining space. Perfect for a balmy day or night.

Dishing the delicious with authentic Thai ingredients

Each dish – from our famous Salt & Pepper Squid to crowd-pleasing Sticky Chicken – is a work of love, exploring Thailand’s richness of natural ingredients from the land and sea and crafted by our expert Thai chefs.
