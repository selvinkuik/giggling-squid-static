With a prime spot in the centre of Weybridge, we’re within easy reach of local shops and picturesque river walks along the River Wey. For those seeking an adrenalin buzz, Thorpe Park is just a ten minute drive!

About Weybridge

Inspired by Thailand’s natural ingredients, our decor is vibrant, floral and as pleasing on the eye as our Thai dishes are on the tastebuds. As stylish as our interior is, we’re all about a friendly, casual atmosphere.

The perfect occasion

Our iconic banquettes are made for sharing dishes with friends and family. We love welcoming our younger guests and have a special kids’ menu offering child-friendly Thai faves so the whole family can feast together.

Outdoor dining

In the summer months, you can book our outdoor tables and enjoy your Thai favourites in the open air, soaking up the sunny day over lunchtime Thai tapas or perhaps enjoying a romantic meal under the stars.

Dishing the delicious with authentic Thai ingredients

Each dish – from our famous Salt & Pepper Squid to crowd-pleasing Sticky Chicken – is a work of love, exploring Thailand’s richness of natural ingredients from the land and sea and crafted by our expert Thai chefs.
