About Oxford

Our lovely Oxford restaurant combines wow-factor decor – bold colours and vibrant botanicals – with a warm atmosphere and friendly service. Not forgetting our food – fresh, authentic and cooked by expert Thai chefs!

The perfect occasion

If you’re looking for a place for lunch or dinner with your student son or daughter, our banquettes are perfect for sharing dishes and giggles. For a special celebration, book our semi-private dining.

Enjoy tapas and cocktails

When the weather is warm, enjoy your Thai feast or some tapas and cocktails outside at our outdoor dining tables. Fresh Thai food and bustling streetlife have always gone hand in hand!

Dishing the delicious with authentic Thai ingredients

Each dish – from our famous Salt & Pepper Squid to crowd-pleasing Sticky Chicken – is a work of love, exploring Thailand’s richness of natural ingredients from the land and sea and crafted by our expert Thai chefs.
